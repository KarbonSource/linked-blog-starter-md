First names in Mien culture is given on the order of their birth

| Born | Male | Female |
| ---- | ---- | ------ |
| 1    | Kao  | Mey    |
| 2    | Nai  | Lai    |
| 3    | San  | Fahm   |
| 4    | Sou  | Fey    |
| 5    | Ou   | Manh   |
Followed by a Personal name given by the father
