# What you need
- proof of identity
- two proofs of residency
- Legal full name document
- social security number

# Applying for a driver's license
- practice driving with a California-licensed driver who is at least 18. This person must sit close enough to take control of the vehicle if needed
- pass a behind-the-wheel test
