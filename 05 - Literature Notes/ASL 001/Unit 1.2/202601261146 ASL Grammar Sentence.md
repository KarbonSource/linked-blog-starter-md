# ASL Grammar Sentence
- Time: this refers to the tense of the sentences (past, present, future)
- Referent: This pertains to the topic and/or the person/object mentioned in the sentences, which may involve more than one element.
- Comment: This includes adjectives, adverbs, or verbs that provide additional information.
- Topic: This is the main subject of discussion.
- Topical Comment: This consists of an adjective, adverb, or noun. That is related to the topic. It cannot be separated from the topic without confusion and must be indicated alongside it.
- Action: This denotes the verb used in the sentence.

- Question: This signifies a word that indicates a question.
- Pluralization: Refers to forming the plural of a signed word. In English, this is done by adding "s" to the noun.
