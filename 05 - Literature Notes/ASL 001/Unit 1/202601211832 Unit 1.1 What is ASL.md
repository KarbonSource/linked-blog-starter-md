# ASL is
An actual language with its syntax and grammar, utterly autonomous from English

OSV (Objective, Subject, Verb)

EX: "I am learning sign language"

Objective - Sign language

Subject - I

Verb - Learn

**ASL is not**

Mime

English on the hands

# SEE I, SEE II
Manually coded English (MCE) system teaches deaf kids English with signs. Not an actual language.

Example: "I am learning sign language"

SVO

I am learn i-n-g **Sign language**

Subject - I

Verb - Learning

Objective - Sign language

# Pidgeon Sign Language
Pidgins often borrow words from their source languages and feature simplified grammar. It's bare-bones language designed to enable minimum-viable communication.

Example: "I am learning sign language"

Follows English order SVO

Me learn Sign Language

Subject - I

Verb - Learning

Objective - Sign language
